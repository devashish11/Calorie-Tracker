### Key Features
* 🧠 **AI Food Description Parser**: Simply type "grilled chicken with brown rice and broccoli" and the integrated Gemini AI instantly calculates the portion sizes, calories, and macros.
* 📸 **AI Photo Meal Analysis**: Snap a picture of your plate or upload an image. The AI recognizes the food items and returns estimated serving sizes and nutrition values.
* ✏️ **Dynamic Calorie Tuning**: Easily override AI estimates with a dedicated inline calorie editor popup featuring real-time input validation.
* ⚙️ **Mobile-First Upload Workflow**: Responsive image input adapts natively to mobile devices for seamless camera capturing or photo gallery picking.
* 📊 **Interactive Daily Log**: Categorize entries into Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, or Snacks, update servings, and track your protein, carbs, fats, and calorie targets.
